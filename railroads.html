Railroads!

Front-end developer for IOS and Android.

Feral Interactive is a British video games developer and publisher for macOS, Linux, iOS, Android and Nintendo Switch. It was founded in 1996 with a focus on bringing games to Mac, it now specialises in porting games to mobile platforms.

I worked with Feral Interactive as a UX Engineer, one of my first projects being Railroads! I coded the interface using RML, a HTML based library which is compatible with C++.

I joined the project in it's final months, receiving UI related bugs from the quality assurance team at Feral. My work would resolve various issues such as text and icons not being centred correctly on all devices, panel's not fitting correctly or localisation bugs.

These bugs were difficult to tackle as I would need to make sure my solution was dynamic and adaptive to possibly much longer lines of text than the original English.

For example, Russian or Japanese translations of the game faced many issues with text overlapping other pieces of information. This was often solved by allowing the text to wrap when possible or else scroll adaptively.
