Total War: EMPIRE

UX Engineer for IOS and Android.

Feral Interactive is a British video games developer and publisher for macOS, Linux, iOS, Android and Nintendo Switch. It was founded in 1996 with a focus on bringing games to Mac, it now specialises in porting games to mobile platforms.

I worked with Feral Interactive as a UX Engineer. The duration of my time here was spent as a lead UX Engineer on Total War: EMPIRE. I coded the interface using a HTML based library which is compatible with C++. I considered a wide range of devices for the development such as different iPhones and iPads aswell as various Android phones and tablets.
