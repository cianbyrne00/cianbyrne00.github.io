Guidelines for a UX designer of a CALL application intended for a Chinese Demographic.

Click here to read my paper.

For my M.Sc. in Interactive Digital Media at Trinity College Dublin I had to write an 8000 word research paper. I was interested in the psychological connection with UX Design.

This Paper explored how the UX/UI design of computer aided language learning (CALL) impacts a language learner's psychology when learning English. Through a combination of primary and secondary sources, guidelines were established for future designers.

The research process consisted of four sections:

  • The gathering of secondary sources and pre-existing data on the subject.
  • The creation of new data via an interview process.
  • Comparing and contrasting the prior data with the researchers new-found data.
  • Establishing guidelines for a UX designer using the previously established.

The paper was awarded a first class honours, the highest award possible for a research paper.
