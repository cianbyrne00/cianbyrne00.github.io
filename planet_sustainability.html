Planet Sustainability.

An Interactive Educational Installation Made in Unreal Engine 5.

For my M.Sc. in Interactive Digital Media at Trinity College Dublin I had to do a research project which explored the realms of digital media.

We decided to create an interactive installation which used the user's body as a controller through motion sensor technology.

The research project was shown to the public in the Science Galery at the heart of Dublin's city centre.
