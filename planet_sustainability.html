Planet Sustainability.

An Interactive Educational Installation Made in Unreal Engine 5.

For my masters at Trinity College Dublin I had to do semester long research project based within interactive digital media. My project saught to explore the theme of climate change using a unique form of interactive via a 3D world.

We decided to create an interactive installation which used the user's body as a controller through motion sensor technology. The most flexable engine to support this ambitious project was Unreal Engine 5. The engine allowing me to programme scriped events, vector based interactions and create a graphically impressive 3D world. I used 3Ds Max to create the 3D models for the world itself.

The research project was shown to the public in the Science Galery at the heart of Dublin's city centre. Many users felt it was a fantastic demonstration of educational based interactive learning.
