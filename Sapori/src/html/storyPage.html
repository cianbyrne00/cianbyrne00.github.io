Sapori

Our Story

We arrived on Pearse Street in 2010. Since then, the whole family has gotten involved. With a great understanding of excellent food Nelly and Yancho started the restaurant to offer Dublin a new gold standard in dining. Not long after Des and Luys, thier sons, joined in to help meet and serve fresh faces everyday.

The Begining

In the begining we were one of the first food and coffee destinations on Pearse Street. Even with the new competition Sapori has stayed competitive and thriving. We knew keeping our food at a high standard whilst making prices fair would be the key to success.

The People

We are a happy family here at Sapori and that doesn't just include the staff! Our service goes beyond the menu. Ensuring all of our customers are looked after like they are one of us is our main objective. Come get to know us and we'll have the usual ready..

The Passion

We are more than just a business. With all of us working together we help uplift eachother and hope to provide that same feeling onto others. With care being brought to everything we do, we want the best for you and our family.
